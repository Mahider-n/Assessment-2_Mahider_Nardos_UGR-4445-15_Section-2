About

At Elsa Kolo, we celebrate the rich tapestry of culinary tradition and modern flavor through our unique range of snacks. Inspired by the vibrant heritage of Ethiopian cuisine, Kolo is our signature offering, crafted from roasted barley (Senfef Gebese) and peanuts, expertly seasoned with a blend of spices that elevate its taste. Our mission is to share the authentic flavors of Ethiopia with the world, providing a delicious and wholesome snacking option for everyone.
